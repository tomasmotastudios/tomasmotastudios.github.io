Privacy

This document establishes the guidelines related to the protection of my privacy and identity, applicable to any digital or in-person interaction.

1. General Principles

  • Respect for individual dignity
  • Consent must be free, informed, and explicit
  • Transparency in the use of personal data
  • Absolute confidentiality of private information

2. Personal Data

The following are considered private and may not be disclosed without explicit authorization:

  • Full name and personal documents
  • Physical or digital address
  • Personal contacts (phone, email, private networks)
  • Real-time or historical geolocation
  • Financial, professional, or academic information not public

3. Image, Voice, and Identity

The use of my image, voice, or any element that identifies me requires prior written authorization. Distorted, commercial, defamatory, or unauthorized use is strictly prohibited.

4. Digital Environment and Content

  • Content created by me must be preserved in its original context
  • Unauthorized use of my data for advertising, AI training, or fake profiles is prohibited
  • Private messages and conversations cannot be made public without authorization

5. Rights

I retain the right to withdraw consent at any time. Any violation of this policy may result in legal action, in accordance with applicable data protection laws (including LGPD and equivalent international laws).

6. Contact

For authorization requests or clarifications, please contact me directly through the official channels I provide.
