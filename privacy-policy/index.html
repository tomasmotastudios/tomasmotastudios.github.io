Privacy Policy

At Tomás Mota Studios (“we”, “our” or “site”), your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information from our visitors and users, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and relevant data protection laws.

1. Data We Collect

  • Voluntarily provided data: name, email, phone, and other information submitted through contact forms, subscriptions, or inquiries.
  • Technical and browsing data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, and geolocation (approximate).
  • Third-party sources: aggregated or anonymized data from analytics or other service providers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • Improve and personalize your experience on the site.
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize content and functionality.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access.
  • Fulfill legal obligations and comply with regulations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be shared only under these circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who support site operations (e.g., hosting, analytics), under confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or court order.
  • To protect rights, safety, or security of the site, its users, or third parties.

4. Data Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Data is stored only for the period necessary to achieve its intended purposes or as legally required.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of personal data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Withdraw consent to specific data processing activities.
  • Request data portability, when technically feasible.

To exercise your rights, contact us through our website form or support email.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, personalize content, and analyze site traffic. For more information, see our Cookies Policy.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page, with its revision date indicated.
