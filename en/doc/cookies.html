Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Tomás Mota Studios uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to recognize preferences and improve functionality.

1. Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: necessary for basic website operations, navigation, and security.
  • Performance / Analytical Cookies: collect anonymous data on site usage to improve performance and content (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: store user preferences like language or layout customization.
  • Marketing / Advertising Cookies: enable targeted campaigns, track ad performance, and provide personalized content.

2. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies allow us to:

  • Maintain essential site functionality and security.
  • Analyze user behavior to optimize content and design.
  • Customize the user experience according to preferences.
  • Deliver relevant advertising when applicable.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality. For advanced management, consult your browser or third-party tools.

4. Consent

By continuing to use the website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page with the revision date indicated.
